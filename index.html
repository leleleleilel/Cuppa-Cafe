About Us

Cuppa Cafe is a coffee haven that embraces the essence of a cuppa, symbolizing a delightful experience. We proudly serve as a coffee shop and eatery, offering a wide range of authentic Indonesian main dishes, delectable snacks, and specialty beverages. Our menu is a celebration of Indonesia's culinary treasures, inviting you to savor the flavors that make our cuisine unique. Join us at Cuppa Cafe for a truly satisfying experience of Indonesian food and coffee delights.
